About Me

I'm a dual-degree student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics at Clayton State University, with an expected graduation in December 2027. My work sits at the intersection of data engineering, machine learning, and statistical modeling — with hands-on experience building end-to-end pipelines using Google Cloud Platform, Python, and SQL. As a Google Cloud Data Engineer Intern, I designed and deployed an object detection model using AutoML Vision and Vertex AI that achieved 95% precision, and built data processing scripts to generate and validate over 500 annotated image datasets. Beyond industry experience, my research projects have focused on mortality forecasting for the Metro Atlanta population, where I engineered predictive pipelines integrating ARIMA and artificial neural networks within the Lee-Carter framework. I'm currently seeking a data engineering internship for Summer 2026, where I can continue applying my technical skills to real-world, data-driven challenges.
