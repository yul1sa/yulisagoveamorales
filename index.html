Research · 2nd Place — STEM Symposium
A Metro Atlanta Study on Mortality Using Lee-Carter Modeling
Our objective is to predict future mortality rates for different age groups in the Metro Atlanta area using a Lee-Carter (LC) Model. We implement Lee-Carter using both Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA) and Artificial Neural Network (ANN). Our models are built on aggregate county-level mortality data from the CDC WONDER Database using years 1979 to 2015. Both models are built using data from 1979–2008, and we reserve years 2009–2015 for validation. We compare performance and error metrics for both models and discuss the implications of these results.
